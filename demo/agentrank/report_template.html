Method. Agentrank reads only what an AI shopping agent can read: the public catalog feed (/products.json), robots.txt directives for seven AI crawlers, JSON-LD structured data on the homepage and product pages, variant and category metadata, and published shipping/return/contact policies. Every check maps to a step where an agent either ranks, describes, or completes a purchase from a store. No login, no private data — if Agentrank can't see it, neither can the agent.

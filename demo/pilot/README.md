# Pilot — pitch demo

A self-contained, single-file interactive demo of **Pilot**, the AI that takes over a
Shopify store and runs it. No backend, no build step — open `index.html` in any browser
(or present from the published artifact link). All data is simulated client-side.

## The 90-second presenter script

Use the **← / →** arrow keys to move between scenes. **↺ Reset** (top right) restarts cleanly.

1. **Connect (01).** "This is a real Shopify store paying for Shopify plus seven apps."
   Click **Take over my store**.
2. **Takeover (02).** Let the log run (~15s). Narrate the right panel: every paid app —
   Klaviyo, Recharge, Gorgias, all of it — gets struck through as Pilot absorbs it.
   The counter ends at **$565/mo replaced**. Then click **See your new store →**.
3. **Your store (03).** The rebuilt storefront. Hit the **Original copy** toggle to show
   the before/after — Pilot inferred the brand voice from 312 reviews and rewrote every word.
4. **Mission Control (04).** The money scene. KPIs tick live, the decision feed streams,
   a support ticket gets answered in front of the audience. Then the mind-blow:
   **approve the price change**, flip back to *Your store* — **the price actually changed**.
   Approve the winning headline — the hero swaps live. "The merchant's job is now this:
   approving three decisions over coffee."
5. Close on the cost panel: **$565+/mo of stack → $99 flat, no take-rate.**

## The pitch in one line

Shopify sells a dashboard a human operates through 20 plugins. Pilot sells an operator.
